Online Semester Classes

BYU Online is included in semester tuition. The courses are the same as the courses on-campus, but you can study in the comfort of your own home.

Enroll Now

Why take online classes?

  • Classes are included in your Fall 2014 tuition
  • Checkpoints with instructiors or TAs help you stay on track
  • Instructors, TAs and free one-on-one tutors are available to assist you
  • Interact with other students through orientation, discussion boards, and webinars

How to Register

Registering for an Online Semester Class is the same as registering for any BYU course, simply login to your myBYU account and add the class through MyMAP.

Important Information

  • You must complete the course by the end of Fall 2014 Semester, courses can be completed as early as October 21, 2014.
  • Online Semester courses are included in tuition at the same per credit hour price as traditional courses.
  • You will attend an orientation meeting at the beginning of the semester to meet your instructor and discuss expectations and requirements.
  • These courses are considered BYU Online Semester Classes and are part of BYU registration.
  • Only the classes listed above are offered for the Fall 2014 Semester.
